About Lixin

I am a coastal physical oceanographer specializing in coastal submesoscale (1-10 km) processes with an interest in interdisciplinary research linking fluid dynamics to biogeochemistry in the coastal environment. I am also a big fan of turbulent and mixing processes in coastal zones. I use a hierarchy of ocean modeling tools, including ROMS (RANS), DIABLO (LES), DEDALUS (CFD solver in spectral methods), and GOTM (turbulence model), to combine with theories to explore coastal oceans. I am currently a graduate research assistant in Oceanography at Texas A&M University working with Prof. Robert Hetland.
